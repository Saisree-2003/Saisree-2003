R&D Expenditure Analysis in India (2005-2010)

Dataset

R&D Statistics 2011-12, Table 17

Source

data.gov.in

Time Period

2005-06 to 2009-10

Tools Used

Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Project Overview

Comprehensive analysis of Research & Development expenditure patterns across different economic sectors in India. This project demonstrates data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and visualization skills using Python's data science ecosystem.

Key Insights

  • Public Administration & Defence shows the highest R&D expenditure
  • Consistent year-over-year growth in overall R&D spending
  • Significant variation in R&D investment across sectors
  • Highest growth observed between 2007-08 and 2008-09
